Galvanized Plain Sheet

A galvanized plain sheet is a type of steel sheet that has been coated with a layer of zinc to prevent corrosion.

The zinc coating protects the steel from rust and other forms of corrosion, which can extend the sheet's lifespan significantly.

Benefits of Using Galvanized plain Sheets

  • Corrosion resistance
  • Durability
  • Low Maintenance
  • Cost-effectiveness

Galvanized Plain Coil/Sheets available in Variety of Sizes, thickness and Shapes:

  • Thickness: 0.16mm to 3.00mm
  • Width: Standard: 910mm, 1000mm, 1055mm, 1400mm, 1500mm
  • Length: Standard: 1830, 2500
  • Brands: AMNS, UTTAM, TATA, JSW
  • Surface finish: Spangle, Mini Spangle, Zero Spangle
